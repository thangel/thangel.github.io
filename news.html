News¶

News on research and teaching matters. Most recent posts are listed below:

  • Quantifying the regional blockage effects of tidal arrays within multi-scale channel network models, posted on 01 January, 2025

    This conference paper explores the regional impacts of tidal arrays on flow blockage using advanced modelling techniques.

  • Operational coastal ocean modelling for tidal stream turbine arrays, posted on 01 January, 2025

    Our development of operational coastal ocean models for simulating tidal stream turbine arrays was published in Applied Ocean Research. Led by Nguyen Q. Chien, this research advances the modelling capabilities for assessing the hydrodynamic impacts of tidal energy installations.

  • On tidal array layout sensitivity to regional hydrodynamics representation, posted on 01 January, 2025

    Our study on the sensitivity of tidal array layouts to different representations of regional hydrodynamics was published in International Marine Energy Journal. This work, led by Connor Jordan, explores how modelling choices affect the optimisation of tidal energy systems.

  • On the economic feasibility of tidal range power plants, posted on 01 January, 2025

    Our investigation into the economic feasibility of tidal range power plants, considering various operational modes and market conditions, was published in Proceedings of the Royal Society A: Mathematical, Physical and Engineering Sciences. This work, led by Konstantinos Pappas, evaluates the potential for tidal range energy to become a competitive renewable energy source.

  • On the economic feasibility of tidal range power plants, posted on 01 January, 2025

    This paper evaluates the economic viability of tidal range power plants, integrating operation optimization and design considerations to assess their potential in the energy market.

  • Modelling the Impact of Structure Generated Shadowing on Tidal Stream Turbine Performance, posted on 01 January, 2025

    This conference paper investigates the effects of turbine foundation shadowing on performance, using CFD to assess cyclic loading and its implications for design.
