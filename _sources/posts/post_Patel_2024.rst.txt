Implementation of homogeneous and heterogeneous tidal arrays in the Inner Sound of the Pentland Firth
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Our research on the implementation of tidal turbine arrays in the Inner Sound of the Pentland Firth, exploring homogeneous and heterogeneous configurations, was published in *Journal of Ocean Engineering and Marine Energy*. This work, led by Misha Patel, contributes to the design and optimisation of tidal energy systems.
