Economics-constrained tidal turbine array layout optimisation at the Putuoshan--Hulu island waterway
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Our study on economics-constrained optimisation of tidal turbine array layouts at the Putuoshan-Hulu island waterway in China was published in *Ocean Engineering*. Led by Can Zhang, this research integrates economic considerations into the design of tidal energy arrays for improved feasibility.
