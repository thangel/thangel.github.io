About us¶

These pages detail information on research activities on applied fluid mechanics research led by Dr Athanasios Angeloudis.

Introduction¶

Athanasios Angeloudis

Lecturer, School of Engineering,
University of Edinburgh

I am working on engineering fluid mechanics at the University of Edinburgh, incorporated within the Institute for Infrastructure and the Environment and leading the Applied Fluid Mechanics Group. Aside from my role of lecturer in Civil Engineering, as an Industrial Innovation Research Fellow of the UK’s Natural Environment Research Council (NERC) I coordinate research activities on computational methods for marine and offshore renewable energy, working closely with industry and other research groups.

I am Chartered with the Technical Chamber of Greece and a member of the Institution of Civil Engineers. In addition, I am a member of NERC’s Peer Review College.

My research and expertise spans across

  • Environmental fluid mechanics

  • Offshore renewable energy

  • Coastal process and related engineering applications

  • Numerical methods in civil & environmental engineering

  • Data analytics and optimisation methods
